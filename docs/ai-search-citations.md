## The 3 layers behind every AI citation

5W Communications consolidated 680M+ AI citations across ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews in May 2026. The data is unambiguous: every citation an AI engine assigns goes through 3 distinct layers. Every brand showing up in AI answers right now has solved all 3.

### Layer 1: retrieval - can the engine see you at all
AI engines don't crawl the internet equally. They pull from a small subset of trusted, structured sources.

The math:
- ChatGPT matches Bing's top 10 results 87% of the time. Miss the Bing first page and ChatGPT functionally can't see you (Virayo, April 2026)
- Google AI Overviews: 76.1% of cited URLs rank in the top 10 organic. AI Overview is built on top of traditional Google rankings
- The top 15 domains capture 68% of all AI citation share. Citation visibility is more concentrated than Google PageRank ever was
- Sites that lost rankings in Google's January 2026 update saw an average 22% drop in AI citations across all engines (Search Engine Journal)
- Traditional SEO literally became the entry requirement.

What to focus on:
- Get to top 20 on Google AND Bing for your core category keywords
- Structure pages with answer-first paragraphs, FAQ schema, clear H2/H3 hierarchy
- Ship original data, statistics, and source citations (Princeton's GEO study found each lifts AI visibility 30-40%)

### Layer 2: source preference - what each engine actually pulls from
Every AI engine has dramatically different source preferences. "Rank on AI search" is a meaningless phrase. You're optimizing for different engines that pick from different places.

The data from 680M+ citations:
**ChatGPT pulls from:**
- 47.9% Wikipedia
- 11.3% Reddit
- 6.8% Forbes

**Google AI Overviews pulls from:**
- 21.0% Reddit
- 18.8% YouTube
- 14.3% Quora

**Perplexity pulls from:**
- 46.7% Reddit
- 13.9% YouTube
- 7.0% Gartner

Claude is 30% more likely to cite bullet-pointed and structured content than the others.
LinkedIn surged to the #1 cited domain for professional queries between November 2025 and February 2026.

Practical implications:
A brand cited heavily in Perplexity might be completely invisible in ChatGPT. Reddit presence matters more for AI Overviews and Perplexity than ChatGPT. Wikipedia presence is uniquely valuable for ChatGPT.

### Layer 3: selection - what makes them pick you specifically
Every AI engine retrieves dozens of candidate sources for any given query. Then it picks 3.

The signals that decide which 3 are:
1. **Brand Mention:** Ahrefs analyzed 75,000 brands and found that unlinked mentions of your brand correlate with AI Overview citations at 3 times the rate of backlinks.
2. **Branded Anchor Text:** Has a 0.527 correlation to citations - significantly stronger than domain rating (0.334).
3. **Recency:** Content older than 3 months drops sharply in AI citations.
4. **Structure:** LLMs are trained on journalism that leads with the conclusion. Answer-first writing is the difference between getting cited and not.

Selection is where 95% of B2B brands fail. They pass retrieval. They show up in source preference. They don't get picked because the structural signals aren't there.
